Why Hunch

Most ideas die after months of building. Flip the order.

The expensive way is to build first and hope. The smart way is to put a real-looking page in front of real people and let the signups tell you the truth — in minutes, not months.

Build first, hope later

  • Months of work before any feedback
  • Launch to silence

Validate first with Hunch

  • A real-looking page + waitlist in minutes
  • Share it, collect real signups
  • Build only what people already want
How it works

Three steps from idea to evidence.

Describe your idea

Tell Claude what you're thinking of building, in one sentence. Hunch handles the strategy, copy, and design.

Get a real-looking landing page

A finished, responsive page with a waitlist email pop-up on every button — picked from 50 design styles so it never looks template-y.

Share it and measure demand

Put it online for free, post the link, and watch the signups. Lots of interest = a hunch worth building. Silence = months saved.

The honest part

A real test, not a fake one.

Hunch will not invent testimonials, statistics, user counts, or scarcity — because validating with fake proof only proves the lie, and costs you your credibility. The page is persuasive because it's well-crafted. The signal is trustworthy because the emails are real.

Quick start · no coding required

Live in a few minutes.

You'll need access to Claude (a Pro or Max plan is the easiest path). Then:

Add the skill to Claude. Download hunch.skill from the repo and upload it in claude.ai → Settings → Capabilities (Skills).
Ask for your page. "Use Hunch to build a validation landing page for [your idea]." Download the HTML it gives you.
Put it online — free. Drag the HTML onto Netlify Drop for an instant live link.
Collect emails — free. Make a form at Formspree, then replace [SET_ENDPOINT] in the HTML with your form URL.
Share & measure. Post the link where your audience is. Every signup is a vote.
